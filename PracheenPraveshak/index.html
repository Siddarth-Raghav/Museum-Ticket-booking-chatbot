Explore the history of India

Discover the rich cultural heritage of India at the National Museum in New Delhi. Established in 1949, this prestigious institution houses over 200,000 artifacts spanning 5,000 years of Indian history. From ancient sculptures and rare manuscripts to exquisite paintings and archaeological treasures, the museum offers a captivating journey through the subcontinent's diverse artistic and cultural traditions.
